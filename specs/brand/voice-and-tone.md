# Voice and Tone

Level8 writing style, terminology preferences, and messaging guidelines.

---

## Voice Attributes

| Attribute | Description | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| **Direct** | Say it plainly. No corporate speak. | ✅ "Claim this task" · ❌ "Express interest in this opportunity" |
| **Confident** | We know this works. | ✅ "Build your portfolio with real projects" · ❌ "This might help you maybe get experience" |
| **Encouraging** | We're on their side. | ✅ "You've got this" · ❌ "Good luck out there" |
| **Concise** | Respect their time. | ✅ "3 tasks match your filters" · ❌ "We found 3 tasks that match the criteria you selected" |

---

## Tone Shifts by Context

| Context | Tone adjustment | Example |
|---------|-----------------|---------|
| **Empty states** | Warmer, motivating | "No claimed tasks yet — your first contribution is one click away." |
| **Errors** | Calm, solution-focused | "Couldn't load tasks. Check your connection and try again." |
| **Success** | Celebratory but brief | "Comment copied! Paste it on GitHub when you're ready." |
| **Onboarding** | Welcoming, clear | "Level8 surfaces design tasks from open source projects. Find one. Claim it. Ship it." |

---

## Writing Do's and Don'ts

### Do:
- Use "you" and "your" — it's about them
- Lead with verbs in CTAs: "Claim," "Filter," "Explore"
- Be specific: "12 new tasks this week" > "New tasks available"
- Match the energy of makers and builders

### Don't:
- Use jargon: "leverage," "synergy," "utilize"
- Over-promise: "Land your dream job!"
- Sound robotic: "The system has detected…"
- Use exclamation points excessively!!!

## Terminology

|--------|-------|

| Claim | Apply, Express interest |
| Designer | User, Customer |
| Open source | OSS (spell it out) |
